Grilled Cheese How-To

By Ben Whitmore

Ingredients:

  • Buttermilk Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Butter
  • Honey Baked Sliced Ham (Optional)

Directions:

First, spread a thin layer of butter on a single side of each piece of bread.

Next, place the bread on your pan, butter-face down, with the burner on low.

Place an even amount of cheese on top of the bread, along with your ham (Optional).

Place your other piece of bread on top, with the butter-side facing up.

After a minute or so, flip your sandwich: be sure to make each side a golden brown. Try not to over-do it, as you may burn your bread if you leave it sitting too long.

Enjoy!
